As we know that in everyday busy lives many pet owners and parents leave their child and pets inside the car on a hot day, 
leaving them unattended like this results in them losing their lives due to heat stroke. To prevent this situation from happening 
our device will detect if someone is in the car as well as measure the temperature and notify the user through the app. 
This reminds the user if they have forgotten anyone inside the vehicle if so then they can promptly take action to save lives.
